About Me

When I was younger, I really enjoyed playing games on my computer, I was fascinated by how the computer screen could come to life and make a game that is so entertaining. This interest in technology led me to pursue Electrical and Electronics Engineering at University, I learned about machine learning and Matlab. Although I thoroughly enjoyed my program and internships, I realized that I was significantly more interested in coding through the few courses that I completed. The ability to come up with creative algorithms to solve challenging problems, and to constantly learn new things, was immensely more satisfying to me. To further pursue this passion, I joined a friend’s software company to further understand the day-to-day undertakings of a software engineer. Afterward, I began learning how to code myself through online tutorials like Harvard’s CS50 and Code Academy’s Intro to JavaScript. All these experiences cemented my passion for programming and led me to join App Academy, a rigorous coding Bootcamp.
