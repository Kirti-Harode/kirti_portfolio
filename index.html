About Me

Growing up, my fascination with computer games sparked my interest in technology and drove me to pursue Electrical and Electronics Engineering at university. While I found immense value in my academic pursuits and internships, my passion for coding emerged through completed coursework. Crafting creative algorithms to tackle intricate challenges and the perpetual learning inherent in coding captivated me deeply. Following my immigration to the States with my husband, I delved into programming languages and data structures & algorithms through online tutorials like Harvard’s CS50 and Code Academy’s JavaScript courses. These experiences solidified my dedication to programming, propelling me to enroll in App Academy Bootcamp in San Francisco, California. During the boot camp, I dedicated 80-100 hours per week to craft numerous full-stack projects, gaining proficiency with technologies spanning Ruby on Rails, React, Redux, SQL, PostgreSQL, HTML, CSS, AWS, JavaScript, TypeScript, Canvas, Mongoose, Express, GitHub, MongoDB, Webpack, and more. My tenure as an Associate Software Engineer at Publica by Integral Ad Science in San Francisco, California provided exposure to React, TypeScript, HTML, CSS, SQL, AWS, Cron jobs, Restful APIs, Go, User Interface, and diverse facets of the application development life cycle. I actively contributed to technical design, test case creation, coding, debugging, peer-review, deployment, post-deploy support, and database maintenance using gorm and SQL, managed cron jobs, worked on many frontend projects, collaborating with teams to implement user-centric functionalities and enhance overall User Experience and User Interface. Reflecting on my year-long journey as a Software Engineer, I've experienced substantial professional and personal growth. However, my decision to return to India has led me to seek opportunities that leverage my skills and further my growth as a Software Engineer.
